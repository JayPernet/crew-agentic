- **Character DNA**: If the "Signature Item" is a gold compass, it MUST appear in every scene description.

- **Frame-to-End**: In music videos, if you describe a close-up ending and the next scene starts as a wide shot without a cut marker, the AI will fail to generate a smooth transition.
