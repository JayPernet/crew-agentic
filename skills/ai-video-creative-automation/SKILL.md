## When to Use

- When building long-form historical narratives for YouTube/Social Media.
- When acting as a Screenwriter for music bands or solo artists.
- When precise visual consistency (Character DNA) is required across multiple scenes.
- When generating prompts specifically for image-to-video or frame-to-frame workflows.

## Common Gotchas

- **Historical**: Always ensure "Prompt 2 - Consistency Lock" is executed before generating scene prompts.
- **Music Video**: The "Last Frame" of a scene MUST be the "First Frame" of the next unless a hard cut is specified.
- **Safety**: Keep all content family-friendly and YouTube-safe (no gore).
